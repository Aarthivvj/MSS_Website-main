mss tambaram

The Seva Sadan has always been attentive to the needs of the community. In response to an earnest appeal from the people of Tambaram, Chennai, the founder’s setup a co-educational school in 1953 which in course of time has developed into an aided Higher Secondary School catering to students from the LKG to the Twelfth Standard.

The Madras Seva Sadan Tambaram campus is spread over 13 acres, with two schools in its premises.

    • -Madras Seva Sadan Higher Secondary School

    • Tamil Medium School
    • Classes from sixth to twelfth standard
    • Government-aided

    • -The Sir & Lady M Venkatasubba Rao School

    • English medium school
    • LKG to the Twelfth Standard
    • Self Financed

The Sir & Lady M Venkatasubba Rao school was started to provide quality education in the English in the Tambaram Locality. Expanding on this vision, the Seva Sadan, in conjunction with the Madras Round Table 42, has built a block of brand-new classrooms to provide impetus to the English Medium School. The Sponsor, was inspired to donate to this Noble cause after experiencing first-hand the support provided by the Seva Sadan Schools, for their special needs niece.

  • Self Financed, English Medium School
  • Madras Round Table 42 sponsored Classrooms
  • Inauguration of New Classrooms

Starting with our assembly and morning prayer, our vibrant school band fills the air with melodious tunes, while Yoga is practiced for physical, mental & spiritual well-being. Our classrooms and labs are equipped with advanced facilities for teaching the prescribed curriculum.

  • Assembly
  • School band
  • Physics Lab
  • Chemistry Lab
  • Biology Lab
  • Computer science Lab

At MSS Tambaram, apart from imparting discipline and teaching the prescribed curriculum, we place emphasis on sports & extracurricular activities in order to create well-rounded individuals.

  • Sports – Criket, Volleyball court
  • Auditorium
  • Yoga

In addition to this, free, vocational training in tailoring, carpentry and electrical science are also offered to students.

  • Vocational Training in Tailoring, Carpentry & Electrical Science

A healthy & nutritious meal, followed by much laughter & play sums up a day in the life of the Schools.

  • Sathu unnavu scheme for children
