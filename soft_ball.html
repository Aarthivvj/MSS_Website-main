Soft ball

Over the years, the Madras Seva Sadan has recognized the importance of sports, in building all-round character of students. In fact, the founders have been active sports members themselves and as a result, the Sadan has made significant strides in developing focussed sports programs and state of the art, sporting infrastructure, across all our campuses. Our focus on sports extends to cricket, archery, basketball, athletics, volleyball, swimming, football and much much more…

  • Mrs Prema Kumar winning Swimming Masters
  • Multipurpose Courts – cricket, archery, football, basketball, volleyball…
  • Dedicated Swimming pool – OCRA
  • 30,000 Sq ft Futsal Court, in heart of the city
  • 1st in the City - integrated, multi-purpose sports hub (proposed)

In 2018, the Madras Seva Sadan Cricket Academy was established in the Sadan’s Tambaram campus, with Astro turf wickets and two center turf wickets. The facilities were build from the scratch to create a world-class turf wicket and pitch. Additional Astro turf wickets with practice nets were also built in the Chetput Campus.

  • Preparing the Turf Wickets at Tambaram
  • Second in the City, after TN Cricket Academy, Chepauk
  • Astro turf wickets at Chetput

Coaches led by a BCCI Level B coach has resulted in our students achieving remarkable success in recent years, by being selected in city and state level cricket teams. The Lady Andal School and Sir Mutha School have also clinched multiple city- and state-level championships since the opening of the Cricket Academy.

  • City & State level players from Seva Sadan

Recently, softball has gained popularity with our students. Stellar coaching, with top-of-the-line equipment, has resulted in our students showcasing their skills in state and national competitions. Over the years, our swimming pool facility, has been consistently producing state and national level champions under the guidance of our NIS trained swimming coach.

  • Softball practice at the Tambaram Campus
  • Coaching and training girls softball team
  • Swimming Pool ORCA at Chetput Campus
  • Championships (won at state and national levels)

The Sadan takes pride in fostering gender equality and inclusivity. We have expanded our sports programs to include girls from our aided schools, and we aspire to be one of the first schools in the city to field an all-girls' team.

  • Basket Ball Team
  • Archery
  • Seva Sadan Chetput girls school practice
  • Free coaching & Gear for aided schools

The Seva Sadan recognizes that sports as an integral part of education, but not at the cost of replacing academics! As a result, the Sadan has opened its doors to athletes from across the state and provide them with the option of completing their education under the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) program. We are proud of our successful alumni, who, while completing their education, have continued to bring laurels to the country by their sporting achievements!

  • Kavin Quintal – 2 times championship winner – NSF 250
  • Deepika Pallikal – Top 10 Ranked, World Squash Champion
  • Joshna Chinnappa – 1st Indian to win British Junior Squash Championship (under 19)

Sports play an integral role in shaping lives and providing newer career opportunities. With this in mind, we have envisioned a state-of-the-art, high-performance multi-sports hub. This hub will boast the best coaches, trainers, and facilities, and aims to nurture champions in various sports, while providing the same facilities for underprivileged students in our schools.
