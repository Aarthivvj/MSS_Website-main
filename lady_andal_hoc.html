Highlights of the institution

with just eight students, it has grown to include an Activity Centre for preschoolers.

Our Activity Centre

It is led by qualified and experienced Montessori trained teachers, ensuring a nurturing environment for your little ones.

Classes IX to XII

we offer education through the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) for student who couldn't continue their education in the formal system for any reason.
